Short Bio

Hello everyone. It's David Alejo, a post-doctoral research assistant from the University Pablo de Olavide since October, 2015.

I received the Telecommunications Engineering degree in 2007, the M. S. in Automatics, Robotics and Telematics in 2010 and obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2015.

My main research interest include coordination on multi-agent systems, which is the main research line of my Ph.D. Thesis, navigation and path-planning, localization in GPS-denied areas and mapping.

I am currently involved in the Echord++ Project, in particular in the PTDI Challenge on Urban Robotics which is focused on the development on a semi-autonomous ground robot for sewer inspection. I have the honor of belong to the SIAR team.
